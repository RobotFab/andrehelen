Old Sturbridge Village

1 Old Sturbridge Village Road, Sturbridge, MA 01566

Located just one mile away, this is the largest outdoor living history museum in New England. Guests can step back into the 1830s to explore historic buildings, meet costumed interpreters, and see working farms and craft shops.

View

Tree House Brewing Company

129 Sturbridge Road, Charlton, MA 01507

One of our absolute favorite spots! Tree House is a world-renowned destination for craft beer lovers, located just 10 minutes away in Charlton. It's a massive, beautiful space with a stunning outdoor pavilion and plenty of indoor seating. We love heading here for their incredible brews and the relaxed atmosphere, and we think you will too.
