What Time Should I Arrive At The Ceremony?

The ceremony starts promptly at 6:30pm. You can arrive from 6:00pm – 6:30pm.

I Have A Food Allergy, Can I Make A Special Request?

Yes — please indicate this on your RSVP. The Publick House takes food allergies and special diets seriously.

Is There Transportation To And From The Hotels To The Ceremony?

The Publick House has accommodations itself. We do not have a shuttle bus to any of the nearby other hotels.

Is There Parking For The Ceremony Or Reception?

Yes — there is ample parking in the back of the Publick House.
